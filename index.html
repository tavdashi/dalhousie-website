Welcome to Dalhousie

Perched along five hills in the Chamba district of Himachal Pradesh, Dalhousie has been a favourite retreat for over a century. Cool pine-scented air, winding forest trails, and a skyline of old colonial-era buildings give the town a gentle, unhurried charm that suits every kind of traveller — whether you're after quiet mountain views, a romantic getaway, or a base for exploring the wider Himalayas.
