Where to Stay

From cosy homestays to mountain-view resorts — Dalhousie has a place for every traveller.

Accommodation for Every Budget

Whether you're backpacking through Himachal, planning a comfortable family holiday, or treating yourself to a luxury mountain escape, Dalhousie's accommodation options span every price range. Here's a general guide to what you can expect at each level.

Budget · ₹800 – ₹2,000 / night

Affordable Stays

Guesthouses and family-run homestays scattered around Subhash Chowk and Upper Bakrota offer simple, clean rooms — often with home-cooked meals included.

  • Basic ensuite rooms, hot water on request
  • Home-cooked breakfast and dinner at most homestays
  • Walking distance to Mall Road and local markets
  • Best for backpackers, solo travellers, and students
Mid-Range · ₹2,500 – ₹6,000 / night

Comfortable Hotels

3-star hotels and well-reviewed guesthouses around Gandhi Chowk and Bakrota Hills provide a step up in comfort, with reliable amenities for families and couples.

  • Private bathrooms, room heating, and Wi-Fi
  • On-site restaurant or room service
  • Some rooms with valley or forest views
  • Best for families and couples wanting comfort without a high price tag
Luxury · ₹7,000+ / night

Premium Resorts

A handful of heritage hotels and resort properties on the outskirts of town offer panoramic Dhauladhar views, spa facilities, and fine dining — ideal for a special occasion.

  • Spacious rooms or cottages with mountain views
  • Spa, multi-cuisine dining, and bonfire evenings
  • Landscaped gardens and quiet, secluded settings
  • Best for honeymooners and travellers wanting a full retreat experience

Booking Tips

A few things to keep in mind when planning your stay in Dalhousie.

Book Ahead for Peak Season

May–June and the Christmas/New Year period get busy quickly — book accommodation a few weeks in advance during these times.

Check Heating Options

In winter, ask whether rooms have heaters or fireplaces, as temperatures can drop well below freezing overnight.

Location Matters

Staying near Gandhi Chowk or Subhash Chowk keeps you close to markets and viewpoints without needing transport.
